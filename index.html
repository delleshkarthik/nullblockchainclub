Always Vigilant


Information Security (InfoSec) is the practice of protecting information by mitigating information risks
In today's world Information Security has become highly important because of massive increase in online users
Cybersecurity has become a necessity to any organisation or government

Who Are We?

We are from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering.
Our main objective is to introduce students to real world problems and usecases in the field of InfoSec.

What We Do

Following our club’s motto, in today’s world of growing cybercrime, we train young minds to be acutely aware of the world of information security and also emergent technologies like Blockchain to contribute in building a Cyber Secure India.

Trainings

We provide training to the students interested in learning more about the world of information security and blockchain. We also aid and guide them in the projects taken up by them.

Workshops

We bridge the technological gap between the curriculum and the industry by conducting workshops with experienced industry professionals.

Hackathons

To experience real world problems and product development,we provide many fast paced competitions to the students in order to up their game in the development field.

Events

Null club organizes many events offering you the opportunity to engage with emerging technologies
in InfoSec through educational seminars, demos, collaboration and networking
